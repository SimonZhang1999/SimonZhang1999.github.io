I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the [Intelligent Robotics Lab](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/research/activity/computer-science/artificial-intelligence/intelligent-robotics-lab) at the [University of Birmingham](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/), supervised by Dr. [Masoumeh Mansouri](https://iranmansoori.github.io/)
. Prior to this, I obtained my B.Eng. degree from [Southwest Jiaotong University](https://en.swjtu.edu.cn/) and M.Sc. degree from the [University of Birmingham](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/). My current research interests focus on multi-robot formation control, multi-robot motion planning, and multi-robot cooperative manipulation.
